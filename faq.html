Helix is a browser-based coding education platform. You write real code in your browser — no setup, no installs, no fluff. Just you, the problem, and the helix spinning forward. We currently offer 90 lessons across 6 languages: Python, JavaScript, HTML & CSS, SQL, C++, and TypeScript.
The first 5 lessons of every language track are completely free, forever. No credit card required. To unlock all 15 lessons per language (90 total), you'll need a Pro subscription. We believe learning should have a real starting point — no gates, no trials, no countdown timers.
Pro gives you access to all 15 lessons per language track (90 total lessons), early access to new languages as we add them, a Pro badge on the leaderboard, and priority support. Monthly at $9/month, or annual at $4.50/month (billed $54/year — 50% off). One price. The whole helix. See plans →
Yes, always. Cancel from your profile page at any time. Your Pro access continues until the end of your current billing period. No questions asked. No dark patterns, no guilt trips — if you need to pause the coil, you can.
Our editor runs your code directly in your browser. Python uses Pyodide (WebAssembly), JavaScript runs via native browser execution, SQL uses sql.js, TypeScript compiles via the TypeScript compiler, and C++ is compiled via GCC through our partner service. No installation required on your end — the helix spins the moment you open the tab.
You need an account to access lessons and track your progress. Creating an account is free and takes under 30 seconds. You can sign up with email and password — and we're adding Google and Apple sign-in soon. Your streak, XP, and progress are all tied to your account, so it's worth spinning one up.
Yes. Your XP, level, streak, and completed lessons are saved to your account. Your progress is always there when you come back, even if you close the browser. The helix never forgets a rotation you've made.
