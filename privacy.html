Privacy Policy.

We respect your privacy. Here's exactly what we collect and why.

1. What We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the platform:

  • Account info: username, email address, and password hash.
  • Learning progress: lessons completed, XP earned, and streak data.
  • Profile data: display name, bio, and profile picture (if you add one).
  • Usage data: which lessons you open and challenge attempts.

We do not collect payment card data — billing is handled entirely by our payment processor.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the Helix platform.
  • To personalize your learning experience and track your progress.
  • To show your progress on the leaderboard — only your username and XP are ever visible, never your email.
  • To send important account notifications (password resets, billing alerts).
  • To improve lesson content based on aggregate completion data.

3. Data Storage

Currently, most user data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. For Pro accounts, session data and progress may sync to our servers.

We use industry-standard encryption for any data in transit.

4. Cookies

We use two categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies for session management (keeping you logged in).
  • Analytics cookies to understand how learners use the platform.

You can disable analytics cookies in your browser settings. We don't use advertising cookies.

5. Sharing Your Data

We never sell your personal data. We may share data only with:

  • Payment processors — for Pro subscriptions, they handle all billing data.
  • Analytics providers — aggregate, anonymized usage data only.
  • Law enforcement — only when legally required.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Access all your data from your profile settings.
  • Delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Danger Zone.
  • Request a data export by emailing support@learnhelix.org.
  • Opt out of analytics by adjusting your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Helix is not directed at children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has created an account, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a child under 13 is using Helix, please contact us at support@learnhelix.org.

8. Changes

We'll update this policy as the platform evolves. We'll notify you of significant changes via the email address on your account.

Continued use of Helix after changes take effect means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@learnhelix.org
